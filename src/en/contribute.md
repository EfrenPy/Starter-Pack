<h1>Contribute to CERN Starter Pack</h1>

      <p>This site is maintained by CERN community members. Your feedback and contributions make it better for the next newcomer.</p>

      <h2>How You Can Help</h2>
      <ul>
        <li><strong>Report outdated information:</strong> If you find any content that is no longer accurate, let us know so we can update it.</li>
        <li><strong>Suggest new resources:</strong> Know an official CERN link or external resource that would help newcomers? We'd love to add it.</li>
        <li><strong>Share your experience:</strong> Your onboarding story can help identify gaps in our guides and improve them for future newcomers.</li>
        <li><strong>Fix errors:</strong> Spotted a typo, broken link, or incorrect detail? Report it or submit a fix directly.</li>
      </ul>

      <h2>Get in Touch</h2>
      <p>Send us an email at <a href="mailto:info@efrenrodriguezrodriguez.com">info@efrenrodriguezrodriguez.com</a> with your suggestions, corrections, or feedback. We will review your message and update the site accordingly.</p>

      <h2>Contribution Guidelines</h2>
      <ul>
        <li><strong>Official sources only:</strong> Please suggest or reference only official, verified sources (CERN, government, institutional).</li>
        <li><strong>Keep it factual:</strong> Avoid personal opinions on employers, landlords, or service providers. Stick to objective, verifiable information.</li>
        <li><strong>Be helpful:</strong> Focus on information that is practical and actionable for someone arriving at CERN for the first time.</li>
      </ul>
